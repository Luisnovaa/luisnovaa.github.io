Drone racing

PV drone racing (where FPV stands for first-person view or first person video) is a sportypically small radio-controlled aircraft or quadcopters), equipped with cameras while wearing head-mounted displays showing the live stream camera feed from the drones. Similar to full size air racing, the goal is to complete a set course as quickly as possible. FPV (first person view) flying means that pilots only see what the drone sees.
